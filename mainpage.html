Data

Metrics4 data management platform and analytical applications are designed to provide efficient data collection, sophisticated data analysis and flexible data provisioning. We have utilized over 20 years of our collective experience in designing and implementing data warehousing & business intelligence solutions, combined with sound investment analysis background, to create this unique platform. Metrics4 is empowered with the most comprehensive, structured, and live database of over 80 discrete types of key corporate events, covering over 4,000 US listed companies.

Key Features of Metrics4 Data:

  • Over 4,000 U.S. listed companies, covering over 98% of the total market cap.
  • Over 80 different event types including: Earnings, Guidance, Dividends, Buybacks, Stock Splits, Spin-offs, M&A, Equity and Debt Offerings, Index Changes, FDA Approvals, Retail Sales, CEO Changes, Board Changes.
  • Over 500 qualitative & quantitative attributes to screen and analyze Events linked to financial impact.
  • Over 2,000,000 events and growing everyday to screen and analyze.
  • Over 100,000 proxy proposals, 15,000 proxy meetings, and thousands of Shareholder Proposals.
  • Over 12 million Mutual Funds proxy voting decisions.
  • Over 30 distinct corporate governance event types and over 50 qualitative and quantitative governance metrics.
  • Over 300 proxy contests, over 200 activist funds managing over $100 billion.

About

Metrics4 Analytics is a unique cloud-based financial data and intelligence platform for investment professionals, investor relations executives, and corporate governance professionals. Over 2,000 organizations including corporations (issuers), stock exchanges, and institutional investors are benefiting from EventVestor, the event-driven analytics and proxy analytics platform powered by Metrics4.

Sophisticated investment strategies require timely information and advanced analytics that clearly illustrate the drivers of security performance and risk. Metrics4’s unique and comprehensive information and investment analytics capabilities provide you with the tools and information to help you make key investment decisions while staying abreast of market fluctuations.

Our goal is to be the leader in providing event-driven insights to institutional investors, investors relations, and corporate governance professionals.

  • Unique technology and process to collect, process, and manage structured and unstructured data.
  • Highly structured and granular event data providing compelling value for both technical and fundamental investors as well as investor relations, corporate governance and other corporate executives.
  • Brings context and clarity to market-moving events for investors and corporate executives.

Our mission is to provide accurate and timely actionable information by bringing structure and relevance to financial and business data, enabling better investment and business decisions, faster.
